## 1. What Are Ads? {#what-are-ads}

Think of it like this:

You want to put a big billboard on the road so people see "Download RentalHub." You'd pay the billboard owner money, right?

**Ads on Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Twitter are the same thing** — you pay them money, and they show your post to people who might want to rent houses.

**You control:**
- How much you spend per day (start small, like ₦2,000/day)
- Who sees it (people in Nigeria, age 22-45, interested in real estate)
- Where they go when they click (your download page)
- How long the ad runs

**You only pay when people see it or click it** — not just for posting it.

## 4. Step-by-Step: Facebook Ads {#facebook-ads}

Facebook and Instagram ads both run through **Meta Ads Manager** (they are owned by the same company).

### Step 1: Create a Meta Business Account
1. Open your browser and go to **https://business.facebook.com**
2. Click **"Create Account"**
3. Enter your business name: **RentalHub**
4. Enter your name and business email
5. Click **Submit**
6. Facebook will send you a confirmation email — open it and click the link
7. Your business account is ready

### Step 2: Set Up Payment
1. In Business Manager, click the **hamburger menu (≡)** in the top-left
2. Go to **Billing** → **Payment Methods**
3. Click **Add Payment Method**
4. Add your card details (Visa or Mastercard works)
5. Set your billing country to **Nigeria**

### Step 3: Create Your First Ad
1. Go to **https://adsmanager.facebook.com**
2. Click the green **+ Create** button
3. Choose your campaign objective → click **"Traffic"** (you want people to visit your download page)
4. Click **Continue**
5. Name your campaign: **"RentalHub App Launch"**
6. Click **Continue** again

### Step 4: Set Your Budget
1. Under **Daily Budget**, enter **2000** (₦2,000 per day)
2. Set the **start date** to today
3. Set the **end date** to 7 days from now (so ₦14,000 total for the test)
4. This means Facebook will show your ad to as many people as ₦2,000/day can buy

### Step 5: Choose Your Audience
1. Under **Locations**, type **Nigeria** and select it
2. Under **Age**, set **22 to 45**
3. Under **Detailed Targeting**, type and select:
   - "Real estate"
   - "Renting"
   - "Property"
   - "House hunting"
4. Under **Placements**, choose **Advantage+ placements** (Facebook decides where to show it — Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed, Instagram Stories, etc.)

### Step 6: Create the Ad
1. Under **Ad Name**, type something like "RentalHub Download Ad"
2. Under **Identity**, connect your Facebook Page (create one called "RentalHub NG" if you don't have one)
3. Under **Ad Setup**, choose **"Single image"**
4. Upload the `og-preview.png` image (your yellow banner)
5. Under **Primary Text**, copy and paste this from the ad copy section above:
   > Verified properties. Secure payments. Zero scams.
   >
   > RentalHub is Nigeria's trusted rental platform — 10,000+ verified listings across 36 states.
   >
   > Download free: https://rentalhub.com.ng/download
6. Under **Headline**, type: **"Download RentalHub Free"**
7. Under **Website URL**, paste this link:
   ```
   https://rentalhub.com.ng/download?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign=app_launch_2026
   ```
8. Under **Call to Action**, choose **"Download"** or **"Learn More"**

### Step 7: Publish
1. Click the green **Publish** button
2. Facebook will review your ad (usually 1-24 hours)
3. Once approved, your ad starts showing to people and you start getting traffic

### Step 8: Check Results
- Go back to **Ads Manager** anytime to see how many people clicked, how much you spent, and how many downloads happened
- If it's working well → increase budget to ₦5,000/day
- If it's not working → pause it and try different ad copy or audience

## 5. Step-by-Step: Instagram Ads {#instagram-ads}

Instagram ads run through the **same Meta Ads Manager** as Facebook.

### How to Run an Instagram-Only Ad
1. Follow Steps 1-5 from the Facebook section above (create account, set budget, choose audience)
2. At **Step 6**, under **Placements**, choose **"Manual placements"**
3. **Uncheck** Facebook, Audience Network, and Messenger
4. **Keep only** Instagram checked (Feed, Stories, Reels)
5. Create the ad the same way as Facebook:
   - Upload `og-preview.png`
   - Primary text: copy from "Facebook / Instagram Feed (Long)" in the ad copy section
   - Headline: "Download RentalHub Free"
   - URL: `https://rentalhub.com.ng/download?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign=app_launch_2026`

## 9. How to Track What's Working (Google Analytics) {#tracking}

### Set Up Google Analytics (GA4)
1. Go to **https://analytics.google.com**
2. Click **Start measuring**
3. Account name: **RentalHub**
4. Property name: **RentalHub Web**
5. Time zone: **Africa/Lagos**
6. Choose **Web** as the platform
7. Website URL: **rentalhub.com.ng**
8. You'll get a **Measurement ID** like `G-XXXXXXXXXX`

### Replace the Placeholder
1. Open `client/public/index.html` in your code
2. Find `G-XXXXXXXXXX` (there are 2 places)
3. Replace both with your real Measurement ID
4. Rebuild and deploy

### What to Check After Ads Are Running
After your ads run for a few days, go to Google Analytics:

1. **Traffic Acquisition** → Shows where your visitors came from
   - You'll see: `facebook`, `instagram`, `tiktok`, `twitter`, `direct`, etc.
   - This tells you which platform brings the most people

2. **Conversions** → Shows who actually registered
   - You'll see: "15 people signed up from Facebook, 8 from Instagram, etc."

3. **Realtime** → See who's on your site right now

### What to Do With This Info
- **Facebook brings 50 clicks but only 2 sign-ups?** → Your Facebook ad needs better text or a better image
- **Instagram brings 30 clicks and 15 sign-ups?** → Instagram is your best platform — spend more money there
- **TikTok brings 100 clicks but 0 sign-ups?** → The link might not work on TikTok, or the audience isn't right — pause and fix

## 10. QR Code — Print & Use Offline {#qr-code}

### What It Is
A black-and-white square code that people scan with their phone camera. When scanned, it opens `rentalhub.com.ng/download`.

### How to Access
Go to **https://rentalhub.com.ng/qr-code** in your browser.

### How to Print
1. Open the page on your laptop
2. Press **Ctrl+P** (or **Cmd+P** on Mac)
3. Choose your printer → Print
4. Or: right-click the QR code → **Save image as** → send to a designer or printer

### Where to Use It
- **Flyers** — hand out at bus stops, markets, estates
- **Posters** — paste in estate agent offices
- **Business cards** — include on the back of your card
- **Event banners** — at property expos, trade shows
- **Shop windows** — partner with agents to display it
- **Receipts/invoices** — add it to any paperwork

### How It Works for the Person Scanning
1. They see the QR code on a flyer/poster
2. They open their phone camera (or a QR scanner app)
3. They point it at the QR code
4. A link pops up on their phone
5. They tap it → download page opens → they tap download
