## 4. How to Create Each Type {#how-to}

### Text-Only Video (Easiest — 5 minutes)

**Tool: CapCut (free, phone or computer)**

1. Download CapCut from your phone's app store
2. Open it → tap **"New project"**
3. Choose **"Stock videos"** → pick a solid color background (dark blue #0f172a)
4. Tap **"Text"** → **"Add text"** → type your first line
5. Choose a bold font (Montserrat or Bebas Neue work well)
6. Set the text duration by dragging the edges of the text clip on the timeline
7. Add the next text at the next timestamp
8. Repeat for all text lines
9. Tap **"Audio"** → **"Sounds"** → pick a free beat/music
10. Export as 1080p

---

### Cartoon Animation (Medium — 30 minutes)

**Tool: Animaker (free tier) or Canva**

1. Go to https://animaker.com → sign up free
2. Click **"Create"** → **"Video"** → choose template size
3. In the left sidebar, click **"Characters"** → pick a Nigerian-looking character
4. Drag the character onto the scene
5. Click **"Actions"** → choose what the character does (walking, talking, frustrated)
6. Add text overlays using the **"Text"** tool
7. Click **"Background"** → pick a scene (street, apartment, office)
8. For each scene, click **"+ Add Scene"** at the bottom
9. Add voiceover: click **"Voiceover"** → record yourself or use text-to-speech
10. Download as MP4

**Alternative in Canva:**
1. Search **"animated video"** in Canva templates
2. Pick a template with characters
3. Replace text with RentalHub ad copy
4. Export as MP4

---

### Real Human AI Avatar (Easy — 10 minutes)

**Tool: HeyGen (free trial)**

1. Go to https://heygen.com → sign up (free trial gives you 1-3 videos)
2. Click **"Create Video"** → **"AI Avatar"**
3. Pick an avatar (choose someone who looks Nigerian or African)
4. Paste the script from the "Real Human" section above into the text box
5. Choose the voice (Nigerian English accent if available)
6. Add text overlays using the **"Text"** tool
7. Click **"Generate"** → wait 2-5 minutes
8. Download the video

**Alternative: D-ID**
1. Go to https://d-id.com
2. Upload a photo of a person (or pick from their library)
3. Paste the script
4. It makes the photo "talk" with lip-sync
5. Download
