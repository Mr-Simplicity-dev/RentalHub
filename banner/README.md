# RentalHub Banner Specs — Create in Canva

## How to Create in Canva (5 minutes per banner)
1. Go to https://www.canva.com → Log in (or sign up free)
2. Click **"Create a design"** (top-right)
3. Click **"Custom size"** → Enter the width and height from the table below (in pixels)
4. Search for **"real estate"** or **"home"** in Canva's template library → pick one with a person/house
5. Replace the text with the text below
6. Change colors to match RentalHub branding (see colors below)
7. Add the RentalHub logo (upload `rentalhub-mark.svg` or `rentalhub-mark.png` from your project)
8. Download as PNG

## Canva Tips
- Search templates: "real estate", "property", "home rental", "apartment"
- Use Canva's **photo library** (free photos of people and houses)
- Keep text **big and bold** — people scroll fast on phones
- Make sure the **most important text** is in the center (safe area)
- For story/reel formats (1080×1920), keep key content in the **middle 60%** (top and bottom get cut off by UI)
